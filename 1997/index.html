1997 Symposium on Interactive 3D Graphics

April 27-30, 1997

Providence, RI

The focus of the symposium was on the frontiers of real-time, interactive 3D computer graphics and multimedia.


The symposium consisted of formal paper sessions and hands-on demonstrations where research groups and vendors demonstrated the state-of-the-art in the field.

Final Program

Registion was limited to 250 participants.
