Instructions for Authors


April 26-28, 1999

Sheraton Colony Square Hotel, Atlanta, Georgia, USA

Sponsored by ACM Siggraph


Congratulations on having your paper accepted to the I3DG '99 Conference. Please follow these procedures in formatting and submitting your paper for publication in the Proceedings:


There are three documents for you to download and use in formatting and submitting your paper, located at http://www.cgrg.ohio-state.edu/~spencer/I3DG99/. They are:

copyright_form.pdf -- ACM Copyright Form (Acrobat)
papers-i3d99 -- Formatting and submission instructions (text)
sample_paper.pdf -- sample paper formatted to guidelines (Acrobat)

Each paper can have one color plate page if you so choose. You don't have to have a color plate page if you don't want one.

Remember -- your camera-ready paper and signed ACM Copyright Form are due to Stephen Spencer (address below) on or before February 2, 1999.

If you have any questions regarding the formatting and submission of your paper, please contact:
