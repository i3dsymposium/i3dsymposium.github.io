1999 ACM Symposium on Interactive 3D Graphics


April 26-28, 1999

Sheraton Colony Square Hotel,
Atlanta, Georgia, USA

RESERVATION PROCEDURE:

To reserve your room at the Conference Hotel, which is the Sheraton Colony Square Hotel in Midtown Atlanta, call their future room reservations desk at 404/892-6000 (you can also call 800/422-7895 for their central reservations desk). Be sure to request the Interactive 3-D Graphics Symposium group rate. Reservations must be placed by March 26th. Any reservation made after that date is subject to availability and may not be at the special group rate.

Room rates are $132 per night for a single or double room. Club Level rooms are an additional $20 per night.

There is much to do and see in Atlanta -- from sports to theater to museums to great restaurants. So come early or stay late and enjoy some wonderful Southern hospitality.
