Interactive Graphics Advisory Board


The Interactive Graphics Advisory Board is being formed to steer the symposium in the future and to help the Interactive 3D Graphics community sharpen its vision and its understanding of research opportunities and educational responsibilities. The board will include influential members of the academic community involved with the I3DG Symposium and industrial members who support the Symposium and are responsible for R&D activities in this area.

Current members of the Interactive 3D Graphics Board include:

  • Paul Borrel, IBM
  • Fred Brooks, The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill
  • Ingrid Carlbom, Lucent Technologies
  • Michael Cohen, Microsoft
  • Frank Crow, Interval Corporation
  • Michael Deering, Sun Microsystems
  • Jim Foley, Mitsubishi Electric
  • Henry Fuchs, The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill

  • Jarek Rossignac, Georgia Institute of Technology
