Volunteers Needed
All Volunteer Slots Now Filled

The ACM Symposium on Interactive 3D Graphics is looking for a limited number of student volunteers. For eight hours of work, you will get:
  • free conference registration
  • proceedings
  • all meals served to the regular attendees (including Monday night dinner)

If you are interested in volunteering, please send email to i3dg@cc.gatech.edu with the following information:
  • Name
  • Address
  • City, State Zip
  • Email address
  • URL
  • Name and email address of your Advisor
  • A few sentences on why this symposium would be beneficial to you

We would like to make our decisions on volunteers by the 29th of March. So get your requests in now.
