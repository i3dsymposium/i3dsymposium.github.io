15th Anniversary

March 19-21 2001

Sheraton Imperial Hotel and Conference Center
Research Triangle Park, North Carolina

Hosted by

The bi-annual ACM Symposium on Interactive 3D Graphics has become a traditional forum for presenting the most innovative ideas, techniques, experiments, and research results in interactive design, viewing, and manipulation of 3D models.

The symposium is run in a single track and offers its 250 participants ample opportunities to experiment with the latest products and prototypes and to interact with academic and industrial experts.

The 1999 Symposium on Interactive 3D Graphics was held April 26 - 29, 1999, in Atlanta Georgia, USA.
