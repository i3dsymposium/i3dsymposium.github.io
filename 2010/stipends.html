Student Stipend Program

Prior year's symposia have supported a number of student stipends, for students new to the I3D community looking to make contacts and get ideas for future work. These stipends were funded thanks to the generosity of prior year's corporate sponsors.

Availability of student stipends to support attendees at the 2010 ACM Symposium on Interactive 3D Graphics and Games will depend on the generosity of our corporate sponsors during these tough economic times.

Should funding become available, further information will be posted here.

If you are interested in sponsoring student attendees, please contact the industry co-chairs or the general co-chairs.
