Travel Information

Attendees may fly to Reagan National Airport (DCA) (the closest airport), Washington Dulles International Airport (IAD), or Thurgood Marshall Baltimore/Washington International Airport (BWI) (the furthest away).

Hotel Parking

The Hyatt Regency offers overnight parking ranging from $12 per night to $20 per night, depending on the day and whether you choose self-service or valet parking.

Arrival From Reagan National (DCA) Airport

Public transit from Reagan National Airport to the Hyatt Regency Bethesda is easily available by riding the Washington Metro. Take the Yellow Line from Reagan National Airport station to Gallery Pl-Chinatown, then transfer to the Red Line to the Bethesda station. The Hyatt Regency Bethesda is located at the Bethesda Metro Center. Currently, the fare for this ride is $3.60.

The Hyatt Regency website claims taxi cabs from National Airport to the hotel range from $45-55. Ridesharing services such as Supershuttle start at around $28 for one person.

Arrival From Washington Dulles (IAD) Airport

Public transit from Washington Dulles to the Hyatt Regency Bethesda is available via a bus (the 5A) to the Washington Metro's Orange Line (at either Rosslyn or L'Enfant Plaza stations). Take the Orange Line to the Metro Center station, then transfer to the Red Line to the Bethesda station. The Hyatt Regency Bethesda is located at the Bethesda Metro Center. Currently, the bus fare is $3.10 and the metro fare is $2.90 (via Rosslyn) or $2.95 (via L'Enfant Plaza).

The Hyatt Regency website claims taxi cabs from Dulles to the hotel range from $55-65. Ridesharing services such as Supershuttle start at around $29 for one person.

Arrival From Baltimore/Washington (BWI) Airport

Public transit from Baltimore/Washington International to the Hyatt Regency Bethesda is available via a shuttle bus (the B30) to the Washington Metro's Green Line. Then, take the Green Line from the Greenbelt station to Gallery Pl-Chinatown, then transfer to the Red Line to the Bethesda station. The Hyatt Regency Bethesda is located at the Bethesda Metro Center. Currently, the bus fare is $3.10 and the metro fare is $4.50.

The Hyatt Regency website claims taxi cabs from Baltimore/Washington International to the hotel range from $65-75. Ridesharing services such as Supershuttle start at around $37 for one person.
