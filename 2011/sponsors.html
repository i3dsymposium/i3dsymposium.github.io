The ACM SIGGRAPH Symposium on Interactive 3D Graphics and Games 2011 is a highly-visible, leading-edge conference for techniques that combine 3D computer graphics with human interaction. I3D continues to focus on the hottest research in 3D game technology, interactive visualization and visual depiction, interactive modeling, user-assisted techniques, and applications. As a corporate supporter you participate in this exciting field and demonstrate leadership in supporting the latest developments in computer graphics research.
