Cancellation Policy
  • Please contact the publicity chair Shan Jiang <sjiang4 (at) ics.uci.edu> to request a cancellation. The registration system does not support online cancellation.

  • Late registration cannot be cancelled.
Hotel Registration

Conference hotel registrations are taken by The Westin South Coast Plaza directly. A special room rate at $155 is being offered. This special rate includes wireless internet access. Group rate available until February 11, 2012. Subject to Availability.

To book a room, you can either
1) Book online by going to The Westin South Coast Plaza IEEE VR/ACM I3D Room Block for reservations within the regular conference period (March 4 - 12, 2012),
or
2) Call the hotel directly (1-888-627-7213) if you plan to extent your stay before and/or after the conference period (March 4 to March 12, 2012).
