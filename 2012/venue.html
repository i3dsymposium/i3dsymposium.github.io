Travel Information

There are three airports in the area: Santa Ana (SNA) - 4.8 km/3.0 miles, Long Beach (LGB) - 32.2 km/20.0 miles, or Los Angeles (LAX) - 61.2 km/38.0 miles. For traveling to the hotel from the airports, there are several options:

  • Driving. The address of the hotel is 686 Anton Boulevard, Costa Mesa. Please check the general directions to the hotel for detailed driving directions.
  • Taxi. The estimated rates (one way) are: $9 from SNA, $45 from LGB and $120 from LAX.
  • Courtesy Shuttle. There is courtesy shuttle service from SNA to the Westin hotel, contact: (714) 540-2500.
  • LAX Shuttle Service. Please check www.primetimeshuttle.com, www.supershuttle.com, or www.shuttlefare.com and search travel from LAX to Westin South Coast Plaza, Costa Mesa, 92626. You can book online before your flight. Please take a print out of the receipt and when you arrive at LAX, outside the airport, show that receipt to use the shuttle.

Railway and bus are also available to the Santa Ana Regional Transportation Center. The travel distance from the station to the hotel is approximately 11.27 km/7.0 miles. The estimated taxi rate getting to and from the hotel is $15.

Hotel Parking

The hotel provides valet service for $28 per day and self-parking for $20 per day in the on-site parking garage. The hotel provides additional 50% discount for self parking for i3D 2012 attendees.

For more detailed information about transportation and parking, please refer to the hotel's Transportation Options page.
