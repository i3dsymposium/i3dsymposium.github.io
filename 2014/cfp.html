ACM Symposium on Interactive 3D Graphics and Games (I3D) 2014

Paper submission deadline: October 22, 2013

Conference Homepage: http://www.i3dsymposium.org
Submission System: http://precisionconference.com/~i3d

Conference Dates: March 14-16, 2014
Location: San Francisco, CA

I3D is the leading conference for real time 3D computer graphics and human interaction. 2014 marks the 28th year since the first conference. We invite you to submit papers across the entire range of topics of interaction, interactive 3D graphics, and games. The fall deadline provides the perfect outlet for your summer work. In addition, GDC will be held right after I3D at the Bay Area, making it an excellent opportunity to attend both conferences.

Topics include, but are not limited to:

  1. Interaction devices and techniques
  2. 3D game techniques
  3. Interactive modeling
  4. Level-of-detail approaches
  5. Pre-computed lighting
  6. Visibility computation
  7. Real-time surface shading
  8. Fast shadows, caustics and reflections
  9. Imposters and image-based techniques
  10. Animated models
  11. GPU techniques
  12. Navigation methods
  13. Interactive visualization
  14. Virtual and augmented reality
  15. User studies of interactive techniques and applications
  16. Sketch-based 3D interaction

Paper submissions should be up to 8 pages in length and adhere to the ACM SIGGRAPH style guidelines. The submission of a video to accompany the paper is encouraged. Papers will be peer-reviewed in a single-blind process and authors notified by e-mail.

In addition, the best I3D papers (about 4 to 6 papers) from 2014 will be invited to submit extended versions to appear in a IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics (TVCG) special edition on I3D 2014.
