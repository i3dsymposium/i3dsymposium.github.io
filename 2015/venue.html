Travel Information

For detailed information about transportation and parking, please refer to the hotel's Maps and Transportation page.


View Larger Map

There are three airports in the area: San Francisco (SFO) (the closest), Oakland (OAK), or San Jose (SJC) (the furthest away). For traveling to the hotel from the airports, there are several options:

  • Driving: The address of the hotel is 1250 Columbus Ave, San Francisco.
  • Taxi: The estimated rates (one way) are: $45 from SFO, and $65 from OAK, and $85 from SJC.
  • Shuttle: SuperShuttle provides services to the hotel from all three airports listed above. The estimated rates (one way) are: $17 from SFO, $70 from OAK, and $79 from SJC. Search for "Marriott - Wharf" as your destination. Other shuttle services are also available. Please check the airport's webpage for a list of transportation options.
  • Bus: SamTrans has two bus routes that connect SFO to San Francisco city -- 292 ($2 into SF, $4 out of SF) and KX (express, $5 one way). From around the corner of Samtrans Mission St & 3rd St stop, you may transfer to MUNI bus 30 northbound ($2 one way), which stops at the hotel (Columbus Ave & North Point St). Please refer to samtrans.com and sfmta.com for bus schedules.
  • Train: Caltrain and BART both have stops within 3 miles from the hotel, from which you can either take the MUNI bus 30 or a taxi to the hotel.

Hotel Parking

The hotel provides valet parking is $50 daily. Parking fee excludes tax and includes in/out privileges.
