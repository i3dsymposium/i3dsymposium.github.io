I3D is the leading conference for real time 3D computer graphics and human interaction.


Thanks to everyone who attended I3D 2016! We had 117 registered attendees, which was the one of the highest attendance rates in years. Please see our awards page to see best paper, best poster, best presentation, and other awards. Also, Hao Chen's keynote slides are now available.

Here are some photographs from I3D 2016, courtesy of Mauricio Vives at Autodesk.

For the first time in nine years, I3D will be returning to the greater Seattle area from February 27 to 28, 2016. We will be on the campus of DigiPen Institute of Technology:


DigiPen is located in Redmond, WA, within a few miles of multiple companies focusing on gaming, VR, computational photography, hardware, mapping, and other areas of applied graphics. Please see the venue page for additional details.

2016 is I3D's 30th year since the inaugural workshop in 1986, and the 20th conference (I3D occurred roughly biennially until 2005). More details on prior conference are available here.

Contact: Please send questions to general@i3dsymposium.org for general inquiries, registration, and sponsorship. Direct queries about paper submissions to papers@i3dsymposium.org and poster submissions to posters@i3dsymposium.org.
