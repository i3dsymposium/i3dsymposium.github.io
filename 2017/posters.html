Final Abstracts: Thursday, January 19, 2017

What to submit?
Poster and demo submissions should consist of an extended abstract in PDF format. The abstract can be one or two pages. The abstract must follow the ACM SIGGRAPH formatting guidelines for poster abstracts (linked below). All extended abstracts will be included in the ACM Digital Library (DL). Note that this still allows you to submit your work as a full paper to another conference or journal (or a future I3D conference).

How to submit?
Please e-mail your submission to

Include a one or two page abstract; other supplementary material (under 25MB) may optionally be attached to your e-mail. If your supplementary material will be larger than 25 MB, please check with the Posters Chair to discuss possible arrangements prior to the submission deadline.
