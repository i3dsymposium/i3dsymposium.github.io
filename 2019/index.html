I3D is the leading conference for real time 3D computer graphics and human interaction.

In 2019, the conference will be held 21-23 May 2019 in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. This marks the 33rd year since I3D's inaugural workshop in 1986, and the 23rd edition of the conference.
