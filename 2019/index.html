I3D is the leading conference for real time 3D computer graphics and human interaction.


In 2019, the conference will be held in Montreal, Quebec, Canada (dates TBD). This marks the 33rd year since I3D's inaugural workshop in 1986, and the 23rd edition of the conference.

News

  • 12 October 2018
    The Call for Papers is now available. We invite you to submit papers across the entire range of topics of interaction, interactive 3D graphics, and games.
