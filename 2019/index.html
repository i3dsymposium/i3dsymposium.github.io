I3D is the leading conference for real time 3D computer graphics and human interaction.


The conference will be held in late May 2019 in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. Details will be posted here when available. Join our mailing list or follow us on social media for updates!

2019 is I3D's 33nd year since the inaugural workshop in 1986, and the 21st conference (I3D occurred roughly biennially until 2005). More details on prior conferences are available here.
