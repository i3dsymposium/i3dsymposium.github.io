Posters

Below is a list of posters accepted to I3D 2019.

Authors with accepted posters will present their posters in-person at the conference, and will also have the opportunity to summarize their work in front of a larger audience during thr posters “fast-forward” session.

Applying ZeroMQ to Realtime Global Illumination Rendering

From Ground to Space: Real-time Rendering of Procedural Planets at Arbitrary Altitudes
Florian Michelic, Michael Kenzel, Karl Haubenwallner and Markus Steinberger
Camera Placement Utility for Dialogue Sequences
Ryan Sharpe and Ann McNamara
Computing Three-dimensional Constrained Delaunay Refinement Using the GPU
Zhenghai Chen and Tiow-Seng Tan
Simulation Interaction between Real and Virtual Objects for Playing Table Tennis with Holograms in Mixed Reality
Ruiqi Zhang, Xiaochun Yang, Bin Wang and Shiyu Liu
An End-to-End Model for Low Resolution Handwritten Digit String Recognition
Wenjing Wei, Jun Guo and Yifeng Ma
Near-Point Convergence Measurement in Binocular VR HMD for Personalized Vergence Conflict Reduction
Youngmin Park, Minkyu Gwak and Sangho Lee
Water Flow Interaction with Dynamic Relief Structures in Video
Yi-Chun Tseng and Sai-Keung Wong
Interactive Soft-body Simulation for Surgical Applications
Nabil Boukadida, Sheldon Andrews and Eric Paquette

Awards

Two poster awards will be given for unpublished works: best poster and best student poster.

All questions about posters and poster presentations should be emailed to I3D 2019 Posters Chair Dan Casas, Universidad Rey Juan Carlos (Spain) posters@i3dsymposium.org.
