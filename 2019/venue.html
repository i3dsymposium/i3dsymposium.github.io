Directions

Unity Technologies' offices are located in one of Montreal's architecural landmarks: Le Nordelec.

Please enter the building from Richardson street on the south-east side of the building.

Take the stairs or the elevator to the third (3rd) floor.

Banquet Venue



Pointe-à-Callière

A cocktail dinatoire will be held the second day of the conference (22 May) at Montreal 360 at Pointe-à-Callière. The venue has some of the most spectacular views of Montreal and the Old Port!

Banquet guests will also have the opportunity to visit the Memory Collector and Fort Ville-Marie exhibits at the Montreal Museum of Archaeology and History.

Location

165 Place d'Youville
Montréal, QC H2Y 2B2

Transportation

Attendees are responsible for their own transportation to and from Unity's Montreal office. There are several options if traveling from the downtown area:

  1. Metro: The closest metro (subway) station is Charlevoix, which is a 10 minute walk from Unity's offices.
  2. Bicycle: Bixi is Montréal's public bicycle sharing system. There are stations located within a few blocks of Unity offices at Rue Shearer and Rue Richardson. Bike paths on the nearby Lachine Canal give easy travel access in the east and west directions.
  3. Taxi / Uber: Cab services and Uber may be booked directly from your smartphone.
