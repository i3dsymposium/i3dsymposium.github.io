I3D is the leading conference for real time 3D computer graphics and human interaction.

I3D 2020 is a virtual conference this year, held the week of September 14-18. Paper presentations will be streamed at the I3D YouTube channel during the conference. The Posters Session will be held in a virtual world, requiring registration. Register here for free to fully participate. Please join the mailing list to be informed of important future conference dates and details.

Open Access I3D'20 Proceedings papers
Open Access PACM CGIT papers

2020 is I3D's 34th year since the inaugural workshop in 1986, and the 24th conference (I3D occurred roughly biennially until 2005). More details on prior conferences are available here.
