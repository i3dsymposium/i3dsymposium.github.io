I3D is the leading conference for real time 3D computer graphics and human interaction.

The date and location for I3D 2020 have not yet been finalized. Details will be posted here when available.

2020 is I3D's 34th year since the inaugural workshop in 1986, and the 22nd conference (I3D occurred roughly biennially until 2005). More details on prior conferences are available here.
