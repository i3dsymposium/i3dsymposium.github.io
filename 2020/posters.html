Posters

On Monday, September 14, each poster author will give a pre-recorded fast-forward of their results, then on Wednesday, September 16 present their posters to attendees in a virtual reality environment (don't worry - you can use your browser, too). You will need to register to enter this space. This space is open now. It doubles as I3D's social room for breaks and after hours.
