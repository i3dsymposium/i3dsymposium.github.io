Program

The virtual conference spans Monday September 14 through Friday September 18, about three hours each day. Keynotes and papers will be streamed at the I3D YouTube channel. All times are United States PDT, Pacific Daylight Time. Register here to be able to attend all sessions and participate in discussions.

Monday, 14 September 2020

9:00 am to 12:35 pm

9:00 am Welcome
9:10 am Posters Fast Forward
9:15 am Keynote: Ming Lin (University of Maryland, College Park)
10:05 am break
10:15 am Papers 1: Collisions and Physics
Session Chair: Paul Kry
11:05 am break
11:15 am Papers 2: Textures and Geometry
Session Chair: Angelo Pesce

Tuesday, 15 September 2020

9:00 am to 12:15 pm

9:00 am Welcome
9:05 am Keynote: Rachel Rose (ILM) and Naty Hoffman (Lucasfilm) (live presentation only)
10:05 am break

Session Chair: Andrea Tagliasacchi
11:25 am break
11:35 am Papers 4: Perception and Performance
Session Chair: Qi Sun
