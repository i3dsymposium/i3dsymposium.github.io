Program

The virtual conference spans Monday September 14 through Friday September 18, about three hours each day. Keynotes and papers will be streamed at the I3D YouTube channel. All times are United States PDT, Pacific Daylight Time. Register here to be able to attend all sessions and participate in discussions.

To add the calendar below to your own Google Calendar, go to "Other calendars", click "+", and subscribe to acm.si3D@gmail.com. Or you can download the ICS format file - double-click or import it to import to your calendar app. One other option: bookmark the link to this calendar

Click any individual event and you can add it to your calendar with the link at the bottom.
