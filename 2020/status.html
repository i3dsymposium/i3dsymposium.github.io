Status

Last updated June 25, 2020

Along with many other conferences this year, we have decided to go virtual for I3D 2020. The conference will be the week of September 14-18. The conference will be free to all attendees and authors. More details will be posted here, as well as to the mailing list, Twitter, and directly to contributors, as these are worked out.

Note that all papers are available to all for free from the ACM Digital Library through June 30th:

  • PACM CGIT 3(1)
  • I3D'20 proceedings

Join our mailing list or follow us on Twitter for updates about the conference. Let us know if you have any questions and concerns, and stay well,

The chairs of I3D 2020
