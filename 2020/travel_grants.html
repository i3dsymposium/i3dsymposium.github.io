Student Travel Grants

New this year, the ACM I3D 2020 organization committee is pleased to offer 5 x $700 student travel grants to help students defray the costs of attending the program. Priority will be given to students with an accepted paper at I3D, as well as students from groups historically underrepresented at the conference. Applicants should be full time students (undergraduate or graduate) and will be expected to attend the full conference. Awardees will receive a partial reimbursement of travel expenses (up to $700) and a free registration.

Important Dates

Applications Due: March 1st, 2020. 8PM PST.

Notifications: March 7th, 2020. 8PM PST.

Application Process

Send an e-mail to dan.casas@urjc.es with subject "I3D2020 Travel Grant Application" and attach one PDF file withe following:

  • A cover letter that includes the student’s full name, school affiliation, and contact information, and the advisor’s full name and contact information (if applicable).
  • A 1 page statement from the student outlining their research area and how it relates to I3D.
  • Student’s CV (max 2 pages).
  • A letter of support from the student’s advisor confirming the student’s academic standing, explaining how the student would benefit from conference attendance, and outlining other resources that will be provided to the student for attending I3D.
