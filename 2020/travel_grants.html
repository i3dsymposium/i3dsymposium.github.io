Student Travel Grants

New this year, the ACM I3D 2020 organization committee is please to offer 5 x $700 student travel grants to help students defray the costs of attending the program. Priority will be given to students with an accepted paper at I3D, as well as students from groups historically underrepresented at the conference.

Applicants should be full time students (undergraduate or graduate) and will be expected to attend the full conference. Awardees will receive a partial reimbursement for conference registration and travel expenses. The expected award is $700. Priority will be given to a student with an accepted paper or poster.
