I3D is the leading conference for real time 3D computer graphics and human interaction. As with last year, this year's virtual conference will be free to attend and watch live. Registration will open soon, which will give you the ability to participate in sessions and social activities.

Please join the mailing list to receive important updates including conference dates, CFP, and other information about I3D 2021 as it becomes available.

2021 is I3D's 35th year since the inaugural workshop in 1986, and the 25th conference (I3D occurred roughly biennially until 2005). More details on prior conferences are available here.
