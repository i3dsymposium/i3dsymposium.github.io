Virtual Posters Session

You must register to attend the posters and demos session. For more details, visit the #posters channel once registered.

Posters were a unique experience, held in a 3D VR space (but you could also use your browser), hosted using Mozilla Hubs. A 60-minute tour through the session, featuring all posters, has been streamed to YouTube. A recording is available here.
