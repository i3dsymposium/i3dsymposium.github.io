I3D is the leading conference for real time 3D computer graphics and human interaction.

Please join the mailing list to receive important updates including conference dates, CFP, and other information about I3D 2022 as it becomes available.

2022 is I3D's 36th year since the inaugural workshop in 1986, and the 26th conference (I3D occurred roughly biennially until 2005). More details on prior conferences are available here.
