Conference Venue


Unity Technologies will host I3D 2023!

We are pleased to announce that I3D 2023 will take place at Unity Technologies in Bellevue, WA, United States.

More details will be provided in the near future

Location

10400 NE 4th St., Suite 900,
Bellevue WA, 98004

Directions

The Unity office is located on the 9th and 10th floors of the Lincoln Square South Building, on the cross-streets of 4th street and Bellevue Way NE.

Entry to the office is obtained through the business elevator bay on the 2nd floor.
