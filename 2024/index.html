I3D is the leading conference for real time 3D computer graphics and human interaction.

2024 is I3D's 38th year since the inaugural workshop in 1986, and the 28th conference (I3D occurred roughly biennially until 2005). More details on prior conferences are available here.

Please join the mailing list to receive important updates including conference dates, CFP, and other information about I3D 2024 as it becomes available.
