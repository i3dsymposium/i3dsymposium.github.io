For the first time in nine years, I3D will be returning to the greater Seattle area from February 27 to 28, 2016. We will be on the campus of DigiPen Institute of Technology:


DigiPen is located in Redmond, WA, within a few miles of multiple companies focusing on gaming, VR, computational photography, hardware, mapping, and other areas of applied graphics. Please see the venue page for additional details.

Conference registration is now open, with early registration rates available until Feburary 4th. Hotel bookings can be made at the Hyatt House, Redmond with the group code g-afcm until Feb 4th February 11th.

2016 is I3D's 30th year since the inaugural workshop in 1986, and the 20th conference (I3D occurred roughly biennially until 2005). More details on prior conference are available here.
