I3D is the leading conference for real time 3D computer graphics and human interaction.


New! To clarify: all conference activities (including registration and meals) will be on the DigiPen campus. For those staying at the Hyatt House, they also offer complementary breakfast. (So you can eat twice!)

New! Full details on Hao Chen's Saturday keynote are available.

New! We will have some shuttles to/from DigiPen from the Hyatt House, though ACM is being slow to get these fully confirmed. Shuttles will be limited (a couple runs in the morning and a couple in the evening), so if you need to leave the conference early on Sunday to get a flight, we suggest you plan transport from DigiPen. We encourage you to check with the hotel front desk when you arrive to find more details on shuttle schedules, and perhaps to book a spot on a particular run. It is also a fairly easy 20-30 minute walk (see our 'high-tech' walking map).

For the first time in nine years, I3D will be returning to the greater Seattle area from February 27 to 28, 2016. We will be on the campus of DigiPen Institute of Technology:


DigiPen is located in Redmond, WA, within a few miles of multiple companies focusing on gaming, VR, computational photography, hardware, mapping, and other areas of applied graphics. Please see the venue page for additional details.

Conference registration is now open, with early registration rates available until Feburary 4th. We have a hotel block at the Hyatt House, Redmond, though block registration ended February 11th.

2016 is I3D's 30th year since the inaugural workshop in 1986, and the 20th conference (I3D occurred roughly biennially until 2005). More details on prior conference are available here.

Contact: Please send questions to general@i3dsymposium.org for general inquiries, registration, and sponsorship. Direct queries about paper submissions to papers@i3dsymposium.org and poster submissions to posters@i3dsymposium.org.
