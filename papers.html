Papers Program

Welcome to the 2016 ACM SIGGRAPH Symposium on Interactive 3D Graphics and Games! 2016 is I3D's 30th year since the inaugural workshop in 1986, and the 20th conference (I3D occurred roughly biennially until 2005). For the first time in nine years, I3D will be returning to the greater Seattle area from February 27 to 28, 2016, on the campus of DigiPen Institute of Technology.

This year's program consists of 20 papers selected from 48 submissions by a papers committee comprised of 49 experts from academia and industry. In addition, we continue our collaboration with IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics and the Journal of Computer Graphics Techniques. Five papers originally published in TVCG and JCGT will be presented in the I3D plenary sessions, and a selection of I3D papers will also be invited to submit extended versions for publication in TVCG and JCGT. We hope these partnerships will continue bringing additional attention to I3D and its publication quality.
