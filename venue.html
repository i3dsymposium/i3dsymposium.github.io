Transportation

The nearest airport is Baltimore-Washington Int'l Airport (BWI), which has good domestic and international connections. Other area airports are Reagan National Airport (DCA) and Dulles Int'l Airport (IAD). However, Reagan and Dulles are significantly farther away from the conference venue.

Make sure to plan your transport from the airport in advance! We encourage you to explore shared rides like GO The Airport Shuttle or SuperShuttle.
